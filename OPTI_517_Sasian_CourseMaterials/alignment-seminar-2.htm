Alignment Seminar

Instructor Ken Castle

 

Students taking the seminar for one credit are expected to email to Dr. Sasian and to Dr. Castle a single PDF file titled AlignmentSeminaryourname2018.PDF by April 30th, 2018. The PDF contents are:

A one page summary for each lecture.

A 5-10 page alignment project summary such as the alignment of a system (hardware or simulation analysis), or the summary of at least 3 alignment papers from the SPIE digital library.

A statement that supports that 45 hrs. of work were put into the alignment seminar.
